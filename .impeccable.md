## Design Context

### Users
Operators, launch planners, and token teams using Autolaunch to review launch setup, monitor launches, and move through post-launch actions. They are technical, task-focused, and usually come from the CLI first.

### Brand Personality
Professional, calm, and precise. The interface should feel trustworthy and deliberate, with small moments of delight that never distract from the job.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refine the existing visual language rather than inventing a new one. Keep the current warm operator-console feel, simplify the page structure, strengthen spacing rhythm, and make key actions easier to scan.

### Design Principles
- Make the primary command obvious within seconds.
- Use space and hierarchy before adding decoration.
- Reuse existing components and patterns instead of introducing a new system.
- Keep delight subtle, fast, and attached to useful moments.
- Favor left-aligned, information-first layouts with clear grouping.
